COVID-19, Business Loans, and Government Expenses



From these datasets, I wanted to ask the following:

1) Where was money going, during the COVID-19 pandemic?
2) How was the government responding to the situation?
3) And how was the city looking out for individuals during this time?



Dataset 1:

Mayor's Office of Management & Budget's 'COVID-19 Loan/Grant Report'

This dataset documented loans given out to small businesses in NYC for financial emergencies during COVID-19. It includes information such as the date the loan was given, the business’s service, the recipient’s name, and zip code. The data is provided by the Mayor's Office of Management & Budget.

Dataset 2:

NYC Independent Budget Office's 'Spending by Agency by Expense Description'

This dataset documents government spending in NYC. It is organized by the specific department, what they described the expense as, and the actual cost. This data was collected by The Independent Budget Office of NYC from city's Financial Management System (FMS).
